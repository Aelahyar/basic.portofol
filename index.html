Education

2013

Islamic Junior High School

In this year, I began my studies at an Islamic junior high school based within a traditional boarding school (pondok pesantren) environment. The school combined general education with deep religious teachings, fostering both academic knowledge and strong spiritual values. Living in the pesantren taught me discipline, independence, and the importance of community. This phase of my education laid a strong moral foundation and shaped much of my personal character and mindset moving forward.

2018

Islamic Boarding High School

In 2018, I continued my education at an Islamic-based high school located within a traditional boarding school (pesantren) environment. During this period, I not only deepened my academic knowledge but also gained a strong foundation in Islamic studies, discipline, and moral values. Living in the pesantren taught me responsibility, time management, and the importance of community. It was a unique and transformative experience that shaped both my character and my mindset as I prepared for higher education and future challenges.

2020

Univesity

In 2020, I pursued my higher education at KH. Abdul Wahab Hasbullah University, located in East Java, Indonesia. As an Islamic-based institution, the university upheld religious values while offering a modern and forward-thinking academic environment. It was here that I was first introduced to the world of programming, sparking my interest in technology and software development. This newfound passion led me to major in Information Systems under the Faculty of Information Technology, where I began building a strong foundation in coding, database systems, and software engineering. This period marked a significant transition in my academic journey, combining faith-based learning with the advancement of digital skills.
